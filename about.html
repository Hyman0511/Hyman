Our Story

Lunch Restaurant was founded in 2010, born from a passion for traditional cuisine. After years of studying abroad, our founder Ming Zhang decided to return to his hometown, combining traditional cooking techniques with modern health concepts to create a dining experience that preserves classic flavors while meeting contemporary dietary habits.

Over the past decade, we have consistently selected fresh, high-quality ingredients and adhered to the business philosophy of "quality first, service paramount," earning the trust and support of numerous customers. Today, Lunch Restaurant has developed into a well-known local dining brand with branches in multiple cities.

We believe that food is not just about taste but also cultural heritage. Through each dish, we hope to convey the essence of traditional Chinese cuisine while showcasing our relentless pursuit of innovation and quality.

Our Features

  • Using fresh organic ingredients to ensure food health and safety
  • Combining traditional cooking techniques with modern innovation
  • Diverse menu options to meet different taste preferences
  • Comfortable and elegant dining environment with quality service
  • Strict food safety management to ensure customer peace of mind
  • Regular menu updates with seasonal special dishes
  • Providing takeout and group ordering services
  • Environmental awareness with biodegradable tableware and eco-friendly packaging
